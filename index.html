  • Total distance of the Appalachian Trail is 2,190 miles.
  • From the Southern terminus to Mt. Katahdin in Baxter State Park Maine, the A.T. travels through 14 different states.
  • The Highest point at 6,643 ft. is Clingmans Dome in Tennessee/North Carolina.
  • The Total elevation gain of hiking the entire A.T. is equivalent to climbing Mt. Everest 16 times.
  • 1 in 4 who attempt to thru-hike successfully completes the journey.
  • Hikers usually adopt or are given "trail names" while hiking the Trail. They are often humorous. Examples are "Save The Snails," "Invisible Bear," and "Fire Bolt".
